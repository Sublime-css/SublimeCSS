Why use Sublime?

SublimeCSS is style agnostic -- you make all the style choices.

Style packs are planned for the future, to make styling eaiser if you don't want to style things yourself.

SublimeCSS is forward compatible, so everything you write today will always be compatible with future updates.

SublimeCSS is currently in beta, so updates will come steadily.

SublimeCSS is lightweight and minimal, meaning shorter downloads and faster websites.
